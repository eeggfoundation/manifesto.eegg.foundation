1. Participants of the EEGG Project agree to comply with this Manifesto: https://manifesto.eegg.foundation
  2. Participants of the EEGG Project acknowledge that a special form of simultaneous ownership of tangible and intangible property has been formed. We call this form of ownership the EEGG.
  3. The principle of the EEGG Project is EEGG trading in the form of a blockchain network NFT and the EEGG.foundation technology.
  4. The EEGG.foundation technology is published as open source.
  5. The main website of the EEGG Project is https://eegg.foundation.
  6. The EEGG.foundation technology enables the creation of galleries displaying third-party EEGGs.
  7. EEGG = ROR + NFT.
  8. An EEGG consists of a tangible object (real object of reality - ROR) located anywhere and the one and only digital image of the tangible object (non fungible token - NFT). The ROR is created by the Author.
  9. The Owner of the NFT is also the Owner of the ROR and therefore of the EEGG.
  10. The ROR is independent of the Owner's location.
  11. The ROR can be exhibited by the Exhibitor anywhere and at any time without notifying the Owner. The Exhibitor must indicate the location of the ROR.
  12. The Author may disagree with the exhibition of the ROR in a place that is not in accordance with his beliefs.
  13. The Owner of the EEGG has the right to assume the physical possession of the ROR if he pays the necessary costs to the Exhibitor.
  14. A Prospective Purchaser of the EEGG will make an offer to purchase the NFT from the Owner of the EEGG using the EEGG.foundation technology.
  15. The Owner, Author, Exhibitor, Intermediary, and the EGGG.foundation are entitled to a commission set forth at the creation of the EEGG.
  16. Physical destruction of the ROR does not affect the ownership of the NFT.
  17. This Manifesto must be visibly displayed on the ROR, either as text or a link.
  18. Terms: Author, Owner, Prospective Purchaser, Exhibitor, Participant, Commission, Gallery, EEGG.foundation, Marketplace, Project, Technologie, Smart Contracts.
